Two modes. One honest product.

You decide when Sift is looking. No hidden background capture. A red dot is visible on the page whenever Research Mode is on.

Tier 1 · Always on

Save Mode

Hover any post on X or LinkedIn and tap Save to Sift. Sift extracts the hook, format, tone, key terms, and author. Your saves become a searchable swipe file organized by hook pattern.

  • Hook classification (listicle, imperative, question, personal story, contrarian…)
  • Author, URL, and timestamp
  • Key-term extraction for later topic search
  • Everything stored locally in your browser
Tier 2 · Opt-in session

Research Mode

Start a session before you open your feed. Sift tracks dwell time, which posts you paused on, and engagement clicks. At the end you get a concrete summary, not a vague report.

  • Dwell time per post (how long it stayed on your screen)
  • Clicks you made: comments, profile, link
  • Hook-pattern frequency across the session
  • Draft post ideas based on what actually held your attention

Honest about what it knows.

A lot of AI products get cringe when they overstate what they know. Sift labels its outputs as signals, not truths.

What Sift captures

  • Visible post text + author handle on X and LinkedIn
  • Dwell time (how long a post stayed on your screen)
  • Clicks you make inside post containers
  • Post URLs for later review

What Sift never captures

  • DMs, settings, or any blocked path
  • Keystrokes outside post-action buttons
  • Full-page screenshots (opt-in only, not in this version)
  • Anything while Research Mode is off

Local-first

All data lives in your browser's local storage. There is no server. Nothing is uploaded unless you export it.

Per-site toggles

Decide which sites Sift can watch. Flip any of them off and Sift stops attaching there immediately.

Export and wipe in one click

Download everything as JSON or wipe all data from the dashboard. Your call.

FAQ

Is this spyware?

No. Sift only activates when you start a Research session or click Save on a post. A red badge is always visible on the page when Research Mode is on. All data stays on your device.

Why only X and LinkedIn?

They're the easiest to do well first. Instagram and YouTube Shorts are on the roadmap once the Research Mode summaries feel solid.

Does it really know why I paused?

It knows you paused. "Why" is always an inference. Sift labels every inference with a confidence score (low / medium / high) instead of pretending to read your mind.

Do I need an AI API key?

Not for the MVP. The core pattern analysis is heuristic. Bring-your-own-key AI summaries are planned as an upgrade, but off by default so nothing leaves your device.
