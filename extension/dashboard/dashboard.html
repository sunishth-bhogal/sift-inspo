Sift
Turn your scrolling into research
No sessions yet. Open X or LinkedIn, click the Sift icon, and flip Research Mode on.
No saves yet. Hover a post on X or LinkedIn and click “Save to Sift.”

What Sift captures

  • Visible post text + author handle on X and LinkedIn
  • Dwell time (how long a post stayed on screen)
  • Clicks you make inside post containers (comments / profile / link)
  • Post URLs for later review

What Sift never captures

  • DMs, settings pages, or anything on a blocked path
  • Keystrokes outside post-action buttons
  • Screenshots (not in this version — opt-in only in the future)

Where it lives

All data lives in chrome.storage.local on this device. There is no server. Nothing is uploaded.
